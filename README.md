# Marketing KPI Performance Dashboard

## Overview
An end-to-end marketing analytics dashboard built in Power BI
analyzing campaign performance across Email, SEO, PPC,
and Paid Social channels over H1 2024.

## Tools Used
- Power BI (main dashboard)
- Power Query (data cleaning)
- DAX (calculated measures)
- Excel (mock dataset)
- Powerpoint (Insights tab)

## Key Features
- Star Schema data model (Fact + Dimension tables)
- 15 DAX measures including ROI%, CPA, CVR, and MoM change
- KPI cards with month over month trend indicators
- Trend analysis across 6 months
- Channel performance comparison
- Strategic insights and recommendations page

## Key Insights Found
- SEO delivered the highest ROI (541%) at a flat $1,200/mo spend
- Email had the lowest CPA at $4.90 vs Paid Social at $23.60
- Total revenue grew +76% from January to June
- Recommended reallocating 20% of Paid Social budget to Email/SEO

## Dashboard Pages
1. Executive Summary — KPI cards + Revenue vs Spend
2. Channel Deep Dive — ROI, CPA, and revenue by channel
3. Trend Analysis — Conversion rate, CPA, ROI over time
4. Insights — Findings and strategic recommendations
